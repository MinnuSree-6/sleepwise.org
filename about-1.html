🌙 About Sleep Calculator

Welcome to Sleep Calculator — a free tool to help you find the perfect bedtime and wake-up time based on 90-minute sleep cycles.

Getting the right amount of sleep is important for your health, energy, and mood. Our calculator helps you wake up feeling refreshed by timing your sleep with your body's natural sleep cycles.

This website was created to help people around the world sleep better — completely free of charge.
